Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Simple plugins take 1-2 weeks, while complex mods can take 4-8 weeks. We provide timeline estimates after reviewing your requirements.

What's your pricing structure?

We offer project-based pricing. Simple plugins start at $50, while more complex projects are quoted individually based on requirements.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes! All projects come with 30 days of free support and bug fixes. Extended support plans are also available.

What Minecraft versions do you support?

We develop for all modern Minecraft versions (1.16+) including the latest releases. Legacy version support is also available.
